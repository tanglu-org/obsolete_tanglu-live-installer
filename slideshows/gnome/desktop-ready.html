Out of the box

  • Firefox, Evolution, Shotwell, LibreOffice and Rhythmbox come pre-installed in Tanglu, so you can get started immediately.
  • User Firefox to browse the web, Evolution to organize your e-mails and LibreOffice to do your office work.
  • Manage your pictures with Shotwell
  • Listen to musik using Rhythmbox, or install a different mediaplayer from the repositories.
  • Popular proprietary applications such as Skype, Picasa, Google Earth and Steam can be installed with one click.
