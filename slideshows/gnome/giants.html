Standing on the shoulders of Giants

  • Tanglu is a Debian-based GNU/Linux distribution derived from and compatible with Debian.
  • Tanglu uses the GNOME desktop environment. It's fast, easy to use, highly configurable and has a very low impact on your system's resources.
  • Our innovations, the rapid development of Debian and the huge Debian package selection make Tanglu one of the most attractive desktop operating systems available to home users.
